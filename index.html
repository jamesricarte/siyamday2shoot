19. EXT. PARK - DAY


Maya’s head is resting on Gian’s shoulder while his arms are wrapped around her. The wind was breezy as they sat on the grass. The long silence is cut off by Maya.

MAYA
Paano ko ‘to sasabihin kay Mama?

She stuttered, tears building up again. Gian sighed heavily, not sure what to reply.

GIAN
Di ko din alam, Mai eh. Hindi naman tayo ready sa ganito.

Maya cries even more.

MAYA
Natatakot ako, Gi.

There is dead silence, only the wind, birds, and children playing can be heard.

GIAN
Ako din Mai. Sobra.
